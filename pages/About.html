About Us

Our Pioneering Vision

ReTech.ed imagines a world where e-waste is not just a problem but an opportunity. Through groundbreaking technology and unwavering dedication to sustainability, we're committed to reshaping the narrative surrounding electronic waste.

How ReTech.ed Works

Harnessing the latest advancements in machine learning, EnviroSwap accurately assesses the value and environmental impact of your e-waste. By recycling with us, you not only contribute to a healthier planet but also earn redeemable credits based on the valuable materials recovered during the recycling process. These credits unlock discounts and vouchers across various third-party platforms, creating a sustainable ecosystem where everyone benefits.

Empowering Responsible Recycling

EnviroSwap seamlessly connects you with trusted recyclers in your vicinity, ensuring that your e-waste is collected and processed responsibly. We've streamlined the entire process to make recycling convenient and hassle-free for you.

Raising Global Consciousness

Knowledge is the catalyst for change. Within the EnviroSwap platform, you'll find comprehensive global data on recycled e-waste elements and the detrimental effects of improper disposal. We're committed to raising awareness and fostering a deeper understanding of the importance of responsible e-waste management.

Together, Let's Make a Difference

Be a part of our community! Join us in taking action and making a positive impact on the environment. Together, we can create a sustainable future.
